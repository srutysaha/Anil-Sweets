ABOUT US

Established in 1992, our shop in Manoharpur, Jharkhand, has been a cherished part of the community for over two decades. We take pride in offering a delectable range of Indian mithai and snacks that have been prepared with love and care. With our FSSAI license ensuring the highest standards of quality and hygiene, we're dedicated to delivering the authentic taste of India to your doorstep. Join us on a delightful culinary journey, and experience the rich flavors that have made us a local favorite for years. Welcome to a taste of tradition at its best.
